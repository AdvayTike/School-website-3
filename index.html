Campus Life


School Library

It is a place to read
The School Library is a place to read peacefully. We go there once in a week to read books. There is a wide selection of books, from pop-up children's books to Novels.


Computer Lab

A place filled of Computers
The Computer Lab has enough Computers for the entire Classroom. we go there sometimes to execute and learn new programs such as Tinkercad, Scratch, MS paint, etc.


Robotics lab

It is a place to learn Robotics
The Robotics lab is where we go to interact with robots, Make new robots, learn code and 3D Printing.

Reliance Foundation Football tournament 2019

Podar’s footballers won the U/14 boy’s category at the Reliance Foundation Football tournament 2019

We take pride in sharing with you that our budding footballers won their first match at the Reliance Foundation Football tournament 2019 today, in the U/14 boy’s category. With a score of 3-0, our boy’s claimed victory against the team from Bhakti Vedanta School. The goals were scored by Viraj Sood – 2 and Veer Punamiya – 1. Well Done Boys!!!
